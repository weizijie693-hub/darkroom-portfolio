ABOUT

摄影是我与这个世界对话的方式。
在取景框里，光线有了形状，时间得以停留。
从城市街角到山海之间，我用镜头捕捉那些
即将消逝的瞬间 —— 一束穿过雾霭的光、
一片被风拂过的影、一段无声的故事。

Photography is my way of conversing with the world.
Within the viewfinder, light takes shape
and time stands still.
From city corners to mountains and seas,
I capture fleeting moments —
light through mist, shadow in motion,
stories waiting to be told.
